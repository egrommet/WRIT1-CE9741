## Nerd Blogs

So-called "nerd blogs" are a great resource for keeping tabs on the data-journalism community. (Don't worry about understanding everything in them.) Here's a short list of some of my favorites:

- [ProPublica Nerd Blog](http://www.propublica.org/nerds)
- [chartsnthings](http://chartsnthings.tumblr.com/)
- [Quartz Things](http://quartzthings.tumblr.com/)
- [The Guardian's Datablog](http://www.theguardian.com/news/datablog)
- [Chicago Tribune News Apps blog](http://blog.apps.chicagotribune.com/)
- [Texas Tribune's nerd blog](http://nerds.texastribune.org/)
- [NPR's News Apps blog](http://blog.apps.npr.org/)
